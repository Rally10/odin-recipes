Peruvian Fish Ceviche

Description

Peruvian ceviche is a dish that is gaining more and more popularity internationally. On December 6, 2023 it was declared by UNESCO as Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity. Besides being tasty, it is very healthy and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of white fish fillet of your preference

    • Common sole
    • Cilus (Corvina drum)

  • 15 large lemons
  • 2 red onions
  • 2 lime peppers
  • 1 tablespoon ground garlic
  • 50 grams of culantro
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make

  1. To begin, wash the fish with plenty of cold water and then cut it into cubes of about 1.5 cm. Then add salt and pepper to taste and let it rest at room temperature or in the refrigerator.
  2. In the meantime, peel and slice the onion and wash it in cold salted water. You can also let it soak for about 10 minutes before rinsing.
  3. Next, squeeze the lemons and reserve the juice in a bowl.
  4. Next, wash the aji limo, remove the seeds and veins and cut it into thin strips or small squares.
  5. Then remove the fish from the refrigerator, add the lime juice, chili lime and chopped culantro. Stir gently until the lime reaches all the fish.
  6. Finally add the onions, give a final stir and adjust the seasoning. Leave to marinate for a maximum of 5 minutes.
  7. Enjoy immediately with the garnish of your choice. Garnish with sliced aji limo or rocoto bell pepper strips.
